Daniel Hernangómez Pérez

Welcome! You have reached the page of Dr. Daniel Hernangómez Pérez. I am a postdoctoral researcher working in the Theory of Excited Materials group at the Weizmann Institute of Science under the supervision of Dr. Sivan Refaely-Abramson.

Previously, I was member of the Computational Condensed Matter Theory group at Universität Regensburg under the supervision of Prof. Ferdinand Evers.

I did my undergraduate at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid and my PhD at the Laboratoire de Physique et Modélisation des Milieux Condensés in Grenoble supervised by Dr. Thierry Champel and Dr. Serge Florens.
