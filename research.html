Daniel Hernangómez Pérez

Research Interests

My research interests span a broad and varied range of topics in condensed matter physics and theory for quantum technologies, including among others:

  • Light Matter Coupling (Excitons, Trions) in Low Dimensional Nanostructures and Molecular Systems.
  • Ab initio Methods: DFT, GW, BSE and Friends for Quantum Transport Applications.
  • High-Magnetic Field Physics in Low Dimensions (Quantum Hall Effects, Spin-Orbit interaction).
  • Many-body Magneto-Optical Properties, Phototransport and Magnetism in Low Dimensional Systems.
  • Single-Molecule Surface Science.
  • Quantum Computation.
