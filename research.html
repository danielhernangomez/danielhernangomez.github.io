Research Interests

My research interests span a broad and varied range of topics in condensed matter physics, including among others:

  • Quantum Hall Effects
  • Spin-Orbit Coupling at High Magnetic Fields
  • Light Matter Interaction in Metallic and Semiconductor Nanostructures
  • Single-Molecule Surface Science.
  • Topological Systems: Transport and Phase Transitions.
  • Ab-Initio Methods: DFT, GW, BSE and Friends for Quantum Transport Applications
